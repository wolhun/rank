FAQs

Still Got Questions?

How do I get started with Rapide?
Simply sign up for a free account, invite your team members, and start creating your first project. Our onboarding guide walks you through the essential features in just minutes.
Can I use Rapide for free?
Yes! Wexo offers a forever-free plan with core task management features. Upgrade to premium plans for advanced analytics, unlimited integrations, and enhanced collaboration tools.
Does Rapide integrate with my existing tools?
Wexo connects with 50+ popular platforms including Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft Teams, Trello, and Asana. Check our integrations page for the complete list.
Is my data secure with Rapide?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption, role-based access controls, and regular security audits. Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties.
Can I import data from other task management tools?
Yes, Wexo supports easy data import from most popular project management platforms. Our migration wizard helps transfer your existing projects, tasks, and team structure seamlessly.
